**The Deep Dark Woods**

> **“I’ve been waiting here for days, tryin’ to catch a break. But the way i’m living, it’s gonna cause my heart to ache” ~ The Deep Dark Woods.**

Ok, ok… so Cap beat me to posting about these guys, so I’m simply going to [point you towards his post](http://www.thankscaptainobvious.net/2009/02/deep-dark-woods-winter-hours-2009.html) rather than regurgitate any of it.

**But seriously… you _need_ to listen to [The Deep Dark Woods](http://www.myspace.com/deepdarkwoods)‘ new release, “Winter Hours”. It is, quite simply, a thing of sheer beauty.**

There’s something about this band. A weariness, and darkness for sure… but somewhere bubbling below it all, a joy that is a pure physical force.

**Listen. Love. Support.**
