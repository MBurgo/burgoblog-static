In possibly some of the [best news](http://www.music-news.com/ShowNews.asp?H=Ian-Brown-and-Liam-Gallagher-join-Peter-Hook-supergroup&nItemID=15745) I’ve heard all year,

> _Peter Hook has signed up Liam Gallagher, Ian Brown and Echo + The Bunnymen frontman Ian Mcculloch to form a new supergroup._
> 
> _The New Order bassist, Hook, formed Free Bass with former The Smiths bass man Mikey Rourke and Primal Scream star Mani after controversially announcing the break-up of New Order earlier this year._
> 
> _The new supertrio decided against having a full-time frontman, and instead have recruited a few big names to sing for them._
> 
> _According to reports the group has recorded a forthcoming album to be released early next year._

Just so that everyone knows, I think I’m in heaven…

**Edit**

I’ve just found out that The Charlatans’ Tim Burgess has just turned in the first vocal performance for the band… Hooks’ description of Burgess’ contribution? “Fucking great”. Can this get any better?
