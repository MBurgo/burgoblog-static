Welcome back to another [Live Music Friday](/category/live-music-friday/) here on Burgo’s Blog. For today’s show, I’ve decided to post one of the most requested sets from my readers: **[Radiohead](/tag/radiohead/), performing live at the BBC Radio Theatre, on April 1, 2008.**

![Live Music Friday - Radiohead, live at BBC Radio Theatre](/wp-content/uploads/031.jpg)

Long-time readers of this blog might remember that when Radiohead’s “[In Rainbows](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000YXMMAE?ie=UTF8&tag=busblmuteraba-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=B000YXMMAE)![](/e/ir)” finally arrived, I was… well, [slightly ambivalent about the album](/2007/10/10/in-rainbows-arrived/). There was something there that didn’t connect with me, as in their past albums. But, despite that, I had the feeling that the album was one which would grow in time.

**And, for me at least… I was right.**

**This set, of Radiohead performing live at the BBC Radio Theatre took place on April 1, 2008**, **and was largely in support of that “In Rainbows” album.** That said, there’s also a smattering of songs from other albums, including “**Airbag**“, “**The Tourist**” and “**Lucky**” (from the album “[OK Computer](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B000002UJQ?ie=UTF8&tag=busblmuteraba-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=B000002UJQ)![](/e/ir)“), and “Everything in its right place” from “[Kid A](http://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B00004XONN?ie=UTF8&tag=busblmuteraba-20&linkCode=as2&camp=1789&creative=9325&creativeASIN=B00004XONN)![](/e/ir)“.

**It’s a great live set, and certainly one for all Radiohead fans… not only the completists.** The highlight for me comes in the three songs “House of Cards”, followed by “Weird Fishes” – the two strongest songs on the “In Rainbows” album, in my opinion – followed up by a wonderful rendition of “Lucky”.  
**Without a doubt… Radiohead are still one of the most important bands of our time.**

**Listen. Love. Support.**
