Aah, [Phoenix](http://www.wearephoenix.com/). They’ve come a long way since their break through as the backing band in a remix of Air’s, “[Kelly Watch the Stars](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LwC1swvlBPs)“, **but I knew from the first time that I heard “[If I Ever Feel Better](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mhwufCg7THM)” from their “United” album that this was a band with whom I would have a life-long love affair.**

And now that I’ve heard their latest single, “**Listztomania**” from upcoming “_Wolfgang Amadeus Phoenix_“… **I know that I was right.**

Man, this is cool…
