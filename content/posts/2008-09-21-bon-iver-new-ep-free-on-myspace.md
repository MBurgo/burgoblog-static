Ok… so this isn’t so much a post as an announcement to all **[Bon Iver](/tag/bon-iver/)** fans. Bon Iver (now more than just Justin Vernon, as [pointed out here](/2008/06/27/bon-iver-on-la-blogotheque-flume/)… now a full band) recently contributed to the **Myspace Transmissions series**; and they’re now offering the entire EP (four songs) for free download. For fans, this is a must. The EP features stunning versions of “**For Emma**“, “**Flume**“, “**Lump Sum**” and “**Blindsided**“. [Head here to download the entire thing](http://www.myspace.com/transmissions).

**That is all.**
