I recently came across this session on the always fabulous [LaundroMatinee](http://www.laundromatinee.com/) site, and simply had to post it. Anyone who’s read this blog for any amount of time will know my love for [Joe Pug](/tag/joe-pug/) (after all, I included him in my “[Best Of 2008](/2008/12/07/burgo-best-of-2008/)” post, and called him the artist I “_most wanted to help make it_“.

So when I stumbled on these videos of Joe performing two as-yet-unreleased tracks, “I’m Not So Sure” & “Bury Me Far From My Uniform” (two tracks which I previously pointed you towards in [this post](/2008/11/17/joe-pug-live-on-nprs-mountain-stage/)), it was a given that I would post them. Take simply beautiful songs from Joe Pug, add in one extremely loud cricket… and you’re in for good times.

**Suffice to say, I’m still enraptured by this kid.**

Below, the videos, and after that the mp3s of the tracks.

**Listen. Love. Support.**
