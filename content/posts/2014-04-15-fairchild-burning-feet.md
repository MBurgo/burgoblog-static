It’s been [about two years since I last wrote about FAIRCHILD](/2012/06/21/fairchild-republic-arcadia/), and in that time the band has gone through a few changes – not only have they dumped the “Republic” part of their name, but their sound has also grown in leaps and bounds. In my previous write-up, I worried that the group sounded too much like Two Door Cinema Club – but this track, “Burning Feet”, has a sound that’s all FAIRCHILD.

Good work, guys.
