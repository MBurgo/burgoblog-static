## Elvis Perkins in Dearland – Weeping Pilgrim

![Elvis Perkins](/wp-content/uploads/elvis-perkins2.jpg)I only recently was exposed to this song, “**Weeping Pilgrim**” from [Elvis Perkins](http://www.elvisperkins.net/), which will appear on the forthcoming “_Help Me To Sing_” compilation album.  
What do you need to know about it? Not much… **save for the fact that it’s a cracking tune of longing and struggle, that is everything music _should_ be. Seriously. There’s an indomnitable spirit to this song that simply cannot be beaten down. Listen to this and love it.**

Listen: [Elvis Perkins in Dearland – Weeping Pilgrims](/wp-content/uploads/Weeping-Pilgrim.mp3)

## Nate from “The Format” Returns With A New Tune

![Fun](/wp-content/uploads/fun2.jpg)One of the most popular posts on this blog – not in terms of comments, because… well… there are none – but in terms of pageviews, is my past post about [The Format breaking up](/2008/02/05/the-format-break-up-sadness-ensues/). **‘Twas a sad day indeed when the talented duo called it quits… but it seems Nate Reuss couldn’t stay away too long, and has popped up again on my radar in a new outfit, simply entitled “[fun](http://www.myspace.com/fun)“**. In their [inaugeral Myspace post](http://blog.myspace.com/index.cfm?fuseaction=blog.view&friendID=402387619&blogID=423209400), Nate wrote:

> _The day after The Format had broken up, I already knew that I couldn’t just call it quits. I wasn’t ready to give up music. I had a handful of songs that I was convinced were the best songs I’d written and I don’t think that I would have been able to live with myself if I had just left them to evaporate somewhere in the back of my mind. I don’t even think I had told my mother of the band’s demise before I made the two phone calls that set the amazing events of the past few months in motion.  
> _

And with that, he was back. The band currently have a demo up on their Myspace page, called “**Benson Hedges**” and it’s… **well, it’s pretty cool. It’s great to hear Nate’s distinctive voice again, and – most importantly – it sounds like he’s really having fun again.**  

Listen: [fun – Benson Hedges (demo)](/wp-content/uploads/Benson-Hedges-demo.mp3)

## Preview of Okkervil River’s new “The Stand Ins” release

![Okkervil River](/wp-content/uploads/okkervil_river-the_stand_ins21.jpg)You know, I freaking love [Jagjaguwar](http://www.jagjaguwar.com/). **Any time a label understands the new world that is internet music and engages music bloggers, it gives me the warm fuzzies all over**. Jagjaguwar are offering up “**Lost Coastlines**” – a track from [Okkervil River](http://www.okkervilriver.com/)‘s forthcoming “**The Stand Ins**” album – for **free download, which I’ve linked to below.**  
I have to say, the album was actually leaked a while ago, prior to mastering, and I heard a few tunes from it… and it’s absolutely kicks ass. **It’s exactly what we need from Okkervil River, and this track demonstrates why.**

**Listen. Love. Support.**

Listen: [Okkervil River – Lost Coastlines](http://www.scjag.com/mp3/jag/lostcoastlines.mp3)
