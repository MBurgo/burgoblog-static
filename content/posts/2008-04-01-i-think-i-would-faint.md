**Ryan Adams & The Cardinals and Oasis are going to be doing a short run of Canadian and American shows together.**

From [Oasisnet](http://www.oasisinet.com/site.php?site=news&idx=791):

> _Oasisinet are happy to announce that Oasis will be performing a short run of Canadian and US shows with special guest [Ryan Adams and the Cardinals](http://www.ryan-adams.com/) prior to their headline appearance at the Virgin Festival Toronto on the 7th September.  
> The band will play their first live show in over two years at Seattle’s WaMu Theater on the 26th August_ _before crossing the border to play shows in_ _Vancouver, Edmonton and Calgary._

And the [Ticketmaster page that broke the news](http://www.ticketmaster.com/artist/1210131).

**You lucky, lucky American and Canadian bastards. My jealousy knows no bounds.**
