I only discovered James Bay about two months ago (thanks to this [absolutely wonderful cover of Haim’s “Forever”](https://soundcloud.com/jamesbaymusic/forever)), but in that two months, I’ve been spinning pretty much everything the guy puts out.

Just last week, Bay released new single, “Running”, on Soundcloud, and I think it just might be my favourite track of his yet. It’s incredibly sincere and initimate, and I guarantee that after a few spins, you won’t be able to resist singing along at the 1.44 mark. Listen below…

_Let It Go_ comes out in the UK on May 13th. No word yet on Australian release, but I’ll keep my ears open.
