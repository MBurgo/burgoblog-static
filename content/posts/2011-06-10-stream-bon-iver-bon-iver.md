**Dudes, stream the new self-titled “Bon Iver” LP below**. Thanks to the [New York Times](http://6thfloor.blogs.nytimes.com/2011/06/09/listen-to-the-new-bon-iver-record/?ref=magazine), you can listen to the whole release, two weeks before its official release on June 21.  
  
  
<iframe src="http://cdn.topspin.net/api/v2/widget/player/78889" id="tsFrame78889" frameborder="0" width="580" height="480"></iframe>

  
  
Thoughts? I’m thinking that 95% of “For Emma, Forever Ago” fans will hate it. Me? **I’m thinking this is one of those albums that takes a few spins before it’s the memory of your favourite girlfriend’s scent. That comfortable, warm smell of strawberry vanilla.**

In other words, give it a chance. And “Michicant” and “Wash.” are both freaking spectacular.
