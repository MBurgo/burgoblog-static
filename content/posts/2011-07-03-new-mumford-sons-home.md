Sophomore album pressure? Looks like Mumford & Sons don’t know the meaning of the term. Despite having that ever-so-slightly weighty debut release hanging over their heads, the boys show no signs of feeling anxiety. First they brought us [that beautiful cover of England](/2011/06/17/mumford-sons-england-the-national-cover/), and now – thanks to [KBCO](http://www.kbco.com/pages/studiocondemand_mr.html?feed=225214&article=8715810) – **we can hear this live take of their new tune, “Home”, which has been getting some spins on their latest tour.**

<!-- embed dropped: flash-object http://player.soundcloud.com/player.swf?url=http%3A%2F%2Fapi.soundcloud.com%2... -->

“Home” will more than likely appear on Mumford’s second LP, which is rumoured to be out by the end of the year.
