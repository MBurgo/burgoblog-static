Hand up to a serious count of nepotism on this one, but here’s one of the latest tracks from my step-bro’s band, “[Fairchild Republic](http://fairchildrepublic.com.au/)“, called “Arcadia”. It’s a pretty cool tune, although the guitar line puts me in mind of Two Door Cinema Club’s kick ass “What You Know” a bit too much for comfort. That said, dudes are onto something and I’ll be watching with interest.
