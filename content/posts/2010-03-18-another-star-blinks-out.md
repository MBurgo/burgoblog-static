I can’t tell you how sad this makes me, but news today reached my ears that **Alex Chilton, singer from personal favourites Big Star, passed away today** at age 59 from a suspected heart attack.

To be honest, it’s been a really shitty last 4 months. First [Chris “Spacewolf” Feinstein passed](/2009/12/18/chris-spacewolf-feinstein-ryan-adams-the-cardinals-bassist-dies/) away. Scarcely a week later, [Vic Chestnutt leaves us](http://www.nytimes.com/2009/12/26/arts/music/26chesnutt.html), highlighting issues in the US health care system. Then [Mark Linkous tragically takes his own life](http://www.rollingstone.com/rockdaily/index.php/2010/03/06/sparklehorses-mark-linkous-takes-own-life/).

And it’s just not f\*cking fair.

**It’s just not fair.**
