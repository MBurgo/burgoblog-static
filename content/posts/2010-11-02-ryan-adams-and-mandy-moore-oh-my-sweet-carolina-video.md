So for those of you who don’t know, [Ryan Adams](/tag/ryan-adams/) recently played at Judd Apatow’s “I Found This Funny” [826la.org](http://826la.org/) benefit gig. **It was Ryan’s first live performance in over a year and a half**… and man, I’ve missed him.

Which is why I was unbelievably stoked when this footage of Ryan in pre-show rehearsals performing “Oh My Sweet Carolina” – aided by the oh-so-sweet tones of wife, Mandy Moore – surfaced on the internet.

**It’s a beautiful thing.**
