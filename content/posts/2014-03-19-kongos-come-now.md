You know, one of these days I’m really going to have to update my “[South African bands you NEED to hear!](/2007/12/07/30-south-african-bands-you-need-to-hear/)” post. After all, it’s nearly 7 years old now, and it’s been 9 or so since I left South Africa, so there’s a lot that’s happened in between, and a lot of new bands I need to hear.

Like KONGOS. This tune, Come With Me Now, actually was released in SA back in 2011, but it took a re-release for the US late last year to bring this to my attention. So, better late then never – add this to your “kick-ass SA tunes” list.
