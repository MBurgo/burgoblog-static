**Who knew the kid from [Kick-Ass](http://www.spike.com/video/kick-ass-theatrical/3293291), Aaron Johnson, could boogie like this?**

Although R.E.M had previously released a “lyrics video” version for Überlin, this piece, featuring Aaron Johnson casually strolling down London’s Brick Lane, is the official video. Directed by Aaron’s fiance, Sam Taylor-Wood, the clip is just one of several “specially commissioned films” for the album, which apparently includes two by the [impossibly talented](http://www.nytimes.com/2011/03/04/nyregion/04franco.html) James Franco.
