This is kind of a whole bunch of cool. Chris Carrabba has jumped around a lot since his Dashboard Confessional days, but he’s never quite managed to get out of that pigeon hole. But with recent release from his new outfit, [Twin Forks](http://www.twinforksmusic.com/), it looks like he might have done just that.

The undeniably catchy “Cross My Mind” comes from the group’s debut LP, which was just released in Australia last month. Check it out at an iTunes store near you.
