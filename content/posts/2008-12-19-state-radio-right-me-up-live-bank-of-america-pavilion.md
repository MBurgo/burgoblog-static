It’s obviously a week for [talking about my favourite bands](/2008/12/17/the-damnwells-still-the-worlds-most-underappreciated-band/). Today, I’m going to post about another favourite band of mine, **[State Radio](http://www.stateradio.com/)**. For more about how much this band means to me, you can check out my [past posts on State Radio here](/tag/state-radio/). If not, **suffice to say that this band has been instrumental in changing my world view**. Without a doubt.

Recently, State Radio released a video on Youtube of them performing their killer track, “**Right Me Up**“, live at the Bank of America Pavilion. **And it’s a special, special moment**. See for yourself below.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/1TzVeulq5xQ" title="YouTube video" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen=""></iframe>

**Is it any wonder I love this band?**

On a closing note:  **Congratulations to Chad from the band for winning the Boston Music Awards’ Humanitarian of the Year Award.** It’s a great recognition of the efforts the band has made so far, but as Chad says, “there’s so much more in store with the [Calling All Crows Foundation](http://callingallcrows.org/)“. Visit the site, and support. **It’s the right thing to do.**
