What could be better then hearing me say, “Hey, you know what? Here’s a new [Frightened Rabbit](/tag/frightened-rabbit/) tune”? Oh, I don’t know. How about, “Hey, you know what? Here’s a new Frightened Rabbit tune… and it features Tracyanne Campbell from Camera Obscura”?

**Yeah. How freakin’ cool is that.**

“Fuck This Place” is off Frightened Rabbit’s new self-titled EP, which – at this stage – is only available for purchase on their current tour.  
Listen below, and then head on over to [Listen Before You Buy](http://www.listenbeforeyoubuy.net/listen/listen-two-new-songs-by-frightened-rabbit-fuck-this-place-featuring-tracyanne-campbell-from-camera-obscura-the-work/) for the full scoop.
