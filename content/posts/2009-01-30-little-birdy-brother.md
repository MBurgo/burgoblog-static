**Little Birdy**

Maybe it’s just because I’m having [some personal issues right now](http://matt-burgess.com/2009/01/sometimes-we-bugger-up-yknow/), but this **new single from [Little Birdy](http://www.littlebirdy.net/), “Brother”, from their upcoming album, “Confetti” is one of the most soul-rending songs I’ve ever heard.**

The track also features Australian music legend, Paul Kelly on harmonica and backing vocals.

This song is killing me, right now.

**Listen. Love. Support.**

Listen: [Little Birdy – Brother](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/Little%20Birdy%20-%20Brother.mp3)

“Confetti” is due out in May. If this track is anything to go by, it’s going to be a must-get.
