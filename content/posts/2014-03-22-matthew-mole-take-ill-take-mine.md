South Africa, you have been seriously holding out on me since I left. Where did Matthew Mole come from? How long have you been hiding this dude from me? Because he’s nothing short of brilliant.

Matthew hails from Cape Town, and released his debut LP, _The Home We Built_, last year, when it – apparently – became an iTunes No.1 album – the first time a native South African had ever reached the top spot. And – apparently – I missed it. If you did too, take this opportunity to put Matthew Mole firmly on your radar.
