My days don’t get much better than when I get to put out posts regarding new Ryan Adams tunes. According [Under The Mag](http://www.undertheradarmag.com/media/shine_through_the_dark_mp3_stream/) where this premiered, “**Shine Through the Dark**” was recorded by Adams post-meltdown, while lying in bed with the flu. Our favourite artist, [Mr DRA](/tag/ryan-adams/) calls it “The first track I wrote after my retirement”.

**I call it simply heavenly.**

Below, stream Adams’ brand new solo acoustic tune. _Shine Through the Dark_ will appear on benefit compilation _Live From Nowhere Near You_ which you can preorder [here](http://greydayproductions.com/store/index.php?main_page=product_music_info&cPath=1_59&products_id=219).
