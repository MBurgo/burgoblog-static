It’s been [four years since I last wrote about Donovan Woods](/2010/10/07/donovan-woods-let-go-lightly/) on this blog, but that doesn’t mean I haven’t been spinning his stuff. Just a couple of weeks ago, he dropped this pearler of a tune, “It Just Got Weird”, on his Soundcloud page. Does this mean another release is soon to follow? No idea, but it’s nice thinking there’s more of Donovan coming.
