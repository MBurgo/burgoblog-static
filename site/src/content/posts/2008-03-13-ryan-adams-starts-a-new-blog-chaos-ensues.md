**Ryan Adams has a new blog. Awesomeness.**

**Ryan Adams** has [a new blog](http://dradamsfilms.com/). He’s only had it for a day, but has posted [14 times already](http://dradamsfilms.com/archive).

Can I just say **_how absolutely awesome this is going to be?_**

I give it a week.

**\*\*\***

_(postscript: don’t take this post the wrong way. Ryan Adams is one of my **absolute heroes**. But the dude is just full of too much creativity, and when he decides to vent it hell-bent, as he has now, it’s like a tidal wave: huge, powerful, awe-inspiring… but ultimately damaging. **But man… this is gonna be one hell of a ride!**)_
