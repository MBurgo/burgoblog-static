If you prefer the big mass posts, then let me know; but I think this might be easier to digest. Today’s aural treat is:

\*\*\*

**Need To Breathe – More Time (With a side helping of Washed by the Water)**

![Need to Breathe - More Time](/wp-content/uploads/needtobreathe.jpg)

**Need to Breathe** first appeared on my radar with their 2006 release, “_Daylight_”. While it was certainly an enjoyable album, portions of it seemed too… well, predictable. After all, these boys played mainstream rock, and they were happy to fit the mould. With their latest release, “**The Heat**”, however, they seem to be stepping back from the preconceived notion of “mainstream rock” and following their own tune.

  
Don’t get me wrong; [Need to Breathe](http://www.needtobreathe.net/) still unabashedly fits into the mainstream rock genre. But that needn’t be an automatic death knell. It seems that songwriter-brothers, Bo and Bear Rinehart **have learnt to appreciate the art of restraint more here, and their music is all the better for it.**

Nowhere is this shown better than in the breakdown section of the first track below, “**More Time**”. It’s a great song, and undeniably catchy.

The second track, “**Washed by the Water**”, shows the band’s Christian roots. Unashamedly a worship song, don’t let that deter you; it’s a rollicking tune that anyone can groove to.
