Ok, so here’s the thing: [audiafauna](http://www.audiafauna.com/) haven’t even got around to releasing their debut LP yet. So this may be considered to be somewhat premature… but after spinning the below track, “Roots” a few times, I’m fairly confident in predicting that **the radio stations will just eat this tune up**. It’s pure radio fodder.

And I don’t mean that in a bad way either. It’s just a ridiculously accessible, incredibly catchy tune. Watch the video below, and download the track for free [over at their bandcamp](http://audiafauna.bandcamp.com/).
