**So… in summary**, we have: **Michael Cera** (from the criminally-under appreciated “_Arrested Development_“, and, of course, the much-hyped “_Juno_“); **seriously cool soundtrack** (including such bands as the _Shout Out Louds_, _Bishop Allen_, _Modest Mouse_, _We Are Scientists_, _Devendra Banhart_ and _Band of Horses_)… and, **correct me if I’m wrong, but that’s John Cho who pops up near the end of the trailer.**

In other words… **yeah, this is going to rock.**
