It’s been nearly a year since I [last mentioned](/2010/11/03/burgos-thursday-mixtape-i-sank-under-the-pressure/) First Aid Kit on this blog, which is far too long considering how much I fell in love with their “Ghost Town” single.

The good news though is that can now change, with the news that Klara and Johanna Söderberg’s sophomore effort, The Lion’s Roar, will drop January 24, 2012; featuring production by Bright Eyes’ Mike Mogis as well as guest performances from Conor Oberst and Nate Walcott.

Below, a teaser trailer for “The Lion’s Roar”. 2012 can’t come fast enough.
