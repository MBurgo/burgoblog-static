Because it’s been a while since my last [Ryan Adams](/tag/ryan-adams/) post, and because [Black Cab Sessions](http://www.blackcabsessions.com/) just posted this a few days ago… well, the timing was too fortuitous to ignore. Below, for your entertainment: Ryan Adams performing “Sinking Ships”, live for a Black Cab Session.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/FIxN213J4D8" title="YouTube video" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen=""></iframe>

Hat’s go off to the cabbie; Ryan [could have been a very intimidating passenger](http://www.blackcabsessions.com/sessions.php?id=1231499191&sort=chronological):

> Jason loved the idea, and on his return looked us up and offered his services for a Session. What is more, he just so happened to be a pretty big Ryan Adams fan. Perfect. **There was a slight concern at the start of the Session though: Ryan gets car sick.** Ryan’s manager warned us of this and asked that, as much as possible, we drive in a straight line. It’s definitely one of the stranger requests that Jason’s ever had, but he did his best while in the back Ryan manfully rose above the nausea to sing his heart out. Hope you enjoy it; Jason certainly did. Best straight line he’s ever driven.
