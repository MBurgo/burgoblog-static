This is a really short note here, but to any of you readers who have enjoyed the stuff you’ve heard from [**J****oe Pug**](/tag/joe-pug/) on this blog so far, you might want to head over to [NPR to stream Joe Pug’s October 5 performance from NPR’s Mountain Stage in full](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=96884277)… **trust me, you won’t regret it.**

The set features two unreleased tracks – “Bury Me Far From My Uniform” (absolutely _killer_!) and “Not So Sure” – as well as “Nation of Heat” and “Hymn #101”.

**Joe Pug… still easily one of my favourite artists for 2008.**

Thanks to Sharyn from [No Door Agency](http://www.nodooragency.com/) for dropping me a line and keeping me updated on Joe’s whereabouts.
