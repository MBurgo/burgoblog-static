![](/wp-content/uploads/mixtape.jpg "mixtape") Alas, my old friend Mixwit is no longer, so I can’t embed this mixtape in a pretty package. But click the “play” icon next to the first track, and the tracks will play one after each other. Easy huh?

## The “I Sank Under the Pressure” mix

**1\. [Crystal Castles & Robert Smith – Not In Love](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/Crystal%20Castles%20-%20Not%20In%20Love%20ft.%20Robert%20Smith.mp3)**

Probably the best thing Robert Smith’s produced in years, and the first time I’ve thanked [Crystal Castles](http://crystalcastles.com/). Absolute heaven. This new version of “Not in Love” is the A-side of a single due out December 6 on Fiction.

**2\. [William Fitzsimmons – So This Is Goodbye (Pink Ganter Remix)](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/So%20This%20Is%20Goodbye%20\(Pink%20Ganter%20Remix\).mp3)**

I’ve [written about William Fitzsimmons](/2008/11/14/william-fitzsimmons-the-sparrow-the-crow/) on this blog before, but it’s been a while since. It’s surprising that it’s a remix from [Pink Ganter](http://www.myspace.com/markusganter) that’s brought me back to his stuff, but I’m glad it did.

**3\. [The Black Keys – Everlasting Light](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/01%20Everlasting%20Light.mp3)**

I don’t really need a reason to include this on this mix, do I?

**4\. [Ben l’Oncle Soul – Seven Nation Army](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/01%20Seven%20Nation%20Army.mp3)**

Just a fun, fun cover. I’m a new convert to [Ben l’Oncle Soul](http://www.myspace.com/oncleben), but his brand of uptempo soul has me nodding my head non-stop.

**5\. [The Mariner’s Children – It Carved Your Name Into The Ground](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/03%20It%20Carved%20Your%20Name%20Into%20The%20Ground.mp3)**

I wrote [about the Mariner’s Children here](/2010/10/10/the-mariners-children-it-carved-your-name-into-the-ground/), so I won’t rehash the post. Suffice to say this song is still getting some heavy rotation.

**6\. [Matthew and the Atlas – I Will Remain](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/01%20I%20Will%20Remain.mp3)**

Another [recent post](/2010/10/24/matthew-and-the-atlas-i-will-remain/), I’ll let you read that to get more background. Still one of my favourites of 2010 though.

**7\. [First Aid Kit – Ghost Town](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/1-06%20Ghost%20Town.mp3)**

Seriously, the moment the vocals kick in in this tune, you just know it’s going to be a beautiful, beautiful track. It’s shocking to think that [both these girls](http://www.myspace.com/thisisfirstaidkit) are still not even twenty.

**8\. [Ragsy – Lonely](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/Lonely%20-%20Ragsy.mp3)**

“You don’t know lonely at all…”. I was hooked right from the first refrain, and I’m seriously loving this tune. “Lonely” appears on [Ragsy’s](http://www.myspace.com/ragsy) 2010 self-titled debut EP, which was released last month.
