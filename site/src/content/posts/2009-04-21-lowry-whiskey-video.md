I was thrilled to receive an email yesterday from Leah at [Engine Room Recordings](http://www.engineroomrecordings.com/) letting me know that Lowry (who I [featured back here](/2009/03/23/lowry-love-is-dead/)) have just released their new music video for their tune, “Whiskey”. Not only do I love the track itself, but I was also fascinated by the story behind the video: Lowry teamed up with [$99 Music Video](http://www.99dollarmusicvideos.com/), a “_new online TV network dedicated to emerging bands and filmmakers who work together to create high-quality original music videos for budgets of $99 or less._“

  
**I’d say Lowry definitely got their $99 worth**… check out the video below.
