One of my favourite tracks off of the Tallest Man on Earth’s 2010 release, “The Wild Hunt” (one of my **[releases of the year](/2010/12/27/12-top-albums-2010/)**, by the way), was the heartbreaking **[Love Is All](/2010/11/06/the-tallest-man-on-earth-love-is-all-video/).**

So there was no way I was not going to post this video of Kristian performing the tune on one of my favourite shows, **[Later… with Jools Holland](/tag/later-with-jools-holland/).**  
**There are few people that can pull off such a captivating performance that is so stripped-bare… but The Tallest Man on Earth is one of them.**
