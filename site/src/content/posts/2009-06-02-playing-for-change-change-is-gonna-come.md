I’ve mentioned the [Playing for Change movement before](/2008/12/10/playing-for-change-stand-by-me/) on this blog. They’ve just released their latest episode, “Change Is Gonna Come”. **And really, words aren’t needed to describe the power of this production.** Instead, I’ll just quote directly from [their site](http://www.playingforchange.com/episodes/9/Change_Is_Gonna_Come), and embed the video below. Past that, **we’ll let the music speak for itself.**

> This performance demonstrates what happens when some of the musicians from “Stand By Me” meet and perform for the first time. This song was recorded live in New Orleans, and features Grandpa Elliot and Clarence Bekker on vocals. Thousands of people witness first hand the soul and conviction of two men who make us all believe a change is going to come.
