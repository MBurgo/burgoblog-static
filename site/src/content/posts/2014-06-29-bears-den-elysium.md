Really digging this single from [Bear’s Den](http://www.bearsdenmusic.co.uk/), “**Elysium**“. Bear’s Den have put out two solid EPs in the last couple of years (_Agape_ in particular was a big favourite around these parts), and are working on releasing their debut album later this year. If _Elysium_ is anything to go by, it should be a cracker.
