Since pretty much the release of their debut album, we’ve all been waiting with bated breath for [Mumford and Sons](/tag/mumford-and-sons/)‘ sophomore release. The band has teased us by road-testing quite a few of the rumoured new tunes while on tour; most recently, we [wrote about “Home” here](/2011/07/03/new-mumford-sons-home/).

They’ve now debuted possibly my favourite of the lot, live on Philadelphia’s Radio 104.5 on Saturday. Marcus calls it “Ghosts That We Knew,” while Ben calls it just “Ghosts.” Whatever the title, I can tell you that it’s simply beautiful. Listen below.
