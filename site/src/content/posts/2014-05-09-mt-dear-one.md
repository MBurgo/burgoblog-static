It was a sad day for the local Brisbane music scene when Yves Klein Blue broke up, not least of which because the break up was compounded by the fact that frontman, Michael Tomlinson, left Aussie shores and headed to London. To be honest, the guy’s awesome, but I wasn’t sure we’d hear him again, this side of the pond.

But turns out the dude’s formed a new band over there, [called MT](http://www.mtmanythings.com/), and they’re all sorts of awesome. Take a listen to their track, Dear One, below.
