**Burgo – Mele Cascade, Vanuatu**

… **and it was great.** Although we were _extremely_ unlucky with weather (ie. it rained **every. single. day.** except for one day, which was overcast), it was still a wonderful holiday filled with wonderful people. I’ll have an update with more details over on my personal blog at [Matt-Burgess.com](http://matt-burgess.com/) sometime within the next week or two.

Keeping this strictly music-related though,  I’m catching up on some random events that occurred while I was offline:

– Yesterday was the anniversary of Elliott Smith’s passing. **Five years on, and it’s still a dark day for me.** Wherever you are, Elliott, I hope it’s wonderful.  
Listen: [Elliott Smith – Between the Bars](http://melodywritings.com/burgotunes/04%20Between%20the%20Bars.mp3)

– Diana from Madmuse dropped me a line to let me know that the video for Bill Madden’s “Unfair” track (which was featured on this blog [here](/2008/09/23/bill-madden-child-of-the-same-god/)) has been released into the wild. I’ve included it below. It’s… **well, I’m not sure it lives up to the track itself**, but it’s still worth watching.

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/9vqvKTFxfSU" title="YouTube video" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen=""></iframe>

– Zane from Amiestreet let me know that “_Brett Dennen and AmieStreet.com have teamed up to bring fans his highly anticipated new album, Hope for the Hopeless, as a special $5 pre-order — with all proceeds going to benefit Brett’s favorite charity, The Mosaic Project, a non-profit dedicated to youth-empowerment and education._“.  
I’ll be featuring a review of the album in the next few days, but I wanted to post this **now** as the charity preorder is running until the 27th October, so there’s only a few days left.

Brett Dennen was [one of the first artists I featured on this blog](/2007/11/23/brett-dennen-aint-no-reason/), so I’m looking forward to reviewing the album sometime within the next week. Watch this space.

– I also noted that my “[South African Bands you MUST hear!](/2007/12/07/30-south-african-bands-you-need-to-hear/)” post has been sparking some interesting discussion in the comments, with my musical taste being questioned more than once. **Heh. I love it when readers swear at me.**

… and there’s still a mountain of emails that I have yet to check, so I promise I’ll get through those within the next week or two. **Hope you all behaved while I was away.**
