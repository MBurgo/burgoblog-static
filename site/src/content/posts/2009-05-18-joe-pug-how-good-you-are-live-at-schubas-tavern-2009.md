My [love for Joe Pug](/tag/joe-pug/) is no secret. If there is one artist who I have ever featured on this blog that I would like to see succeed, it’s Joe. Joe’s [“Nation of Heat” EP](/2008/06/18/joe-pug-nation-of-heat-a-thoroughly-impressive-debut/) was passed around my friends and family and random people I accosted on the street like no other, and it was one of the most promising debuts I have heard to date.

Recently, a live show of Joe Pug performing at Schuba’s Tavern on May 1st was posted to archive.org, and the set has pretty much been spinning non-stop through my headphones since then. A pleasant mixture of solo and full band stuff, the show hints at a great upcoming release. The full show [can be downloaded here](http://www.archive.org/details/joepug2009-05-01.aud.flac16), but below, I’ve included the highlight of the set for me… that’s the opening tune, “How Good You Are”.

This song has hit me like a stake through the chest. I simply cannot wait for the upcoming full-length. Here’s to more of this from Joe Pug.

Listen. Love. Support.
