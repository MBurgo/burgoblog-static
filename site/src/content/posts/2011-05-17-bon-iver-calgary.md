As [recently hinted](/2011/04/19/51-seconds-was-all-it-took/) at, I’m quite excited about the new [Bon Iver](/tag/bon-iver/) album. And this first single, “Calgary,” has left me no less excited.

It hints at the new direction that Justin and Co. are heading in (and in fact recalls some of the older DeYarmond Edison sounds for me, actually)… and is the perfect teaser for the upcoming album, _Bon Iver_, due out June 21.

You can [preorder the album here](http://scdistribution.com/boniver/), but in the meantime, enjoy the free (for an email address) download of the track below.
