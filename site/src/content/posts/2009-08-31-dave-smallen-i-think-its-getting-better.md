There is no better day than today, a Monday, to play this song for you. Below, [Dave Smallen](http://davesmallen.com/)‘s “I Think It’s Getting Better”.

You know, I can count on one hand the songs this year that have made me feel just as damn _happy_ as this song has. And I think that maybe… just maybe… Dave’s right: **things really just might be getting better.**

**It’s one of the most beautiful things I’ve ever heard.**
