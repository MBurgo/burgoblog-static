In which Kristian Mattsson, aka [The Tallest Man on Earth](/tag/the-tallest-man-on-earth/), steps out from behind the guitar and in front of a piano, and – a very rare occurence – adds a backing band. **Oh, and debuts a new song, possibly titled “There’s No Leaving Now”.**

All of which comes courtesy of a surprisingly good quality live recording of his recent show in Gothenberg.

I love this.
